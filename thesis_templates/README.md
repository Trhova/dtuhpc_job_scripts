# Thesis Templates

General DTU thesis submission templates for reuse.

- [Markdown template](phd_thesis_submission_template.md)
- [Word template](phd_thesis_submission_template.docx)

The template focuses on reusable front matter, preface sections, abstracts, acknowledgements, publication/output lists, declarations, and a short thesis overview. Add project-specific chapters and manuscripts separately in the final thesis document.
